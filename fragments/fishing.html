Fishing

The Torne River water is so clean we can drink it straight.

It is also one of Europe’s best fishing waters. Trout, Grayling, Whitefish, Perch and Pike are some of the natural varieties you may catch.

Time & Price

  • Price: SEK 995/person
  • Available from 15 Jun – 1 Sept
